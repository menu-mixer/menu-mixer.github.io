## Menu: Drinks

### Recipe: Greek Frappe

## Metadata
- price: 8.00
- tags: vegetarian

## Ingredients
- Greek Nescafe instant coffee
- Sugar to taste
- Ice
- Water
- Chocolate syrup for rim

## Description
A traditional Greek iced coffee with a modern twist. Frothy, refreshing, and served in a chocolate-rimmed cup.

## Instructions
1. Add Greek Nescafe and sugar to a shaker or frappe mixer
2. Add a small amount of water
3. Froth vigorously until thick and foamy
4. Rim glass with chocolate syrup
5. Fill glass with ice
6. Pour frothed coffee over ice
7. Top with cold water to desired strength

### Recipe: Brown Butter Brulee Latte

## Metadata
- price: 7.50
- tags: vegetarian

## Ingredients
- Double espresso
- Housemade brown butter brulee syrup
- Steamed milk

## Description
Rich espresso with housemade brown butter brulee syrup, topped with silky steamed milk. Available hot or iced.

## Instructions
1. Pull double shot of espresso
2. Add brown butter brulee syrup to cup (1.5 oz)
3. Pour espresso over syrup and stir
4. Steam milk to 150-160°F with microfoam
5. Pour steamed milk over espresso mixture
6. Optional: drizzle extra syrup on top

## Notes
Brown butter brulee syrup: brown butter + caramelized sugar + vanilla

### Recipe: Toasted Marshmallow Matcha Latte

## Metadata
- price: 7.50
- tags: vegetarian

## Ingredients
- Ceremonial grade matcha (2 tsp)
- Housemade toasted marshmallow syrup (1 oz)
- Milk (10 oz)
- Hot water (2 oz)

## Description
Creamy matcha latte with housemade toasted marshmallow syrup. A unique sweet and earthy combination.

## Instructions
1. Sift matcha powder into cup
2. Add hot water (not boiling, ~170°F)
3. Whisk until smooth with no lumps
4. Add toasted marshmallow syrup
5. Steam milk and pour over matcha mixture
6. Stir gently to combine

### Recipe: Poached Pear London Fog

## Metadata
- price: 7.50
- tags: vegetarian

## Ingredients
- Earl Grey tea (1 bag or 1 tbsp loose)
- Housemade poached pear syrup (1 oz)
- Steamed milk
- Honey drizzle

## Description
A cozy twist on the classic London Fog, featuring housemade poached pear syrup and a honey drizzle.

## Instructions
1. Steep Earl Grey in 4 oz hot water for 3-4 minutes
2. Remove tea bag/strain leaves
3. Add poached pear syrup and stir
4. Steam milk with light foam
5. Pour steamed milk over tea
6. Drizzle honey on top

### Recipe: Breakfast Sandwich

## Metadata
- price: 10.00

## Ingredients
- Housemade sweet potato biscuit OR croissant
- 1 egg
- Turkey sausage patty
- White cheddar cheese slice
- Fresh fruit for serving

## Description
Hearty breakfast sandwich on housemade sweet potato biscuit or croissant with egg, turkey sausage, and white cheddar. Served with fresh fruit.

## Instructions
1. Warm biscuit or croissant in oven (325°F, 3-4 min)
2. Cook turkey sausage patty until internal temp reaches 165°F
3. Fry or scramble egg to customer preference
4. Slice biscuit/croissant in half
5. Layer: bottom half, egg, sausage, cheese, top half
6. Serve immediately with fresh fruit on the side

### Recipe: Brown Sugar French Toast Bake

## Metadata
- price: 9.00
- tags: vegetarian

## Ingredients
- Brioche bread cubes
- Brown sugar
- Eggs
- Milk
- Vanilla extract
- Cinnamon
- Butter
- Fresh fruit for serving
- Maple syrup

## Description
Baked French toast with brown sugar, served with fresh fruit and maple syrup.

## Instructions
1. Cube brioche bread and place in buttered baking dish
2. Whisk eggs, milk, vanilla, cinnamon, and half the brown sugar
3. Pour mixture over bread, refrigerate overnight
4. Top with remaining brown sugar and butter pats
5. Bake at 350°F for 35-40 minutes until golden
6. Serve with fresh fruit and maple syrup

- Handful arugula
- Pickled red onion (2 tbsp)

- Everything seasoning

## Description

5. Top with arugula and pickled red onion

### Recipe: Hummus Wrap
