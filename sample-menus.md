# Butternut Squash Soup

## Metadata
- prep_time: 20
- assembly_time: 45
- cost: 4.50
- price: 12.00
- tags: vegan, gluten-free

## Ingredients
- 2 lbs butternut squash, cubed
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 medium onion, diced
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 4 cups vegetable broth
- 1 tsp cumin
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Description
Creamy autumn soup with warming spices. A customer favorite during fall and winter months.

## Instructions
1. Roast squash at 400F for 25 minutes
2. Saute onion and garlic in olive oil
3. Add squash and broth, simmer 15 minutes
4. Blend until smooth, season to taste

## Notes
Can substitute pumpkin. Add coconut milk for extra richness.

# Mushroom Risotto

## Metadata
- prep_time: 10
- assembly_time: 35
- cost: 5.80
- price: 18.00
- tags: vegetarian, gluten-free

## Ingredients
- 1.5 cups arborio rice
- 8 oz mixed mushrooms
- 4 cups vegetable stock
- 1/2 cup white wine
- 2 tbsp butter
- 1/4 cup parmesan
- 2 shallots, minced
- Fresh thyme

## Description
Rich and creamy Italian classic with local foraged mushrooms when available.

## Instructions
1. Saute mushrooms until golden, set aside
2. Toast rice with shallots
3. Add wine, stir until absorbed
4. Add stock one ladle at a time, stirring constantly
5. Finish with butter, parmesan, and mushrooms

# Thai Coconut Curry

## Metadata
- prep_time: 15
- assembly_time: 25
- cost: 7.00
- price: 17.00
- tags: vegan, gluten-free, dairy-free

## Ingredients
- 1 can coconut milk
- 2 tbsp red curry paste
- 1 block firm tofu, cubed
- 1 cup broccoli florets
- 1 red bell pepper, sliced
- 1 cup snap peas
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp brown sugar
- Fresh basil

## Description
Fragrant and spicy curry served over jasmine rice. Heat level adjustable.

## Instructions
1. Press and cube tofu, pan fry until crispy
2. Simmer curry paste in coconut cream
3. Add vegetables, cook until tender-crisp
4. Season with soy sauce and sugar
5. Garnish with Thai basil

## Notes
Sub chicken or shrimp +$3. Extra spicy available on request.

# Chocolate Lava Cake

## Metadata
- prep_time: 15
- assembly_time: 14
- cost: 2.80
- price: 10.00
- tags: vegetarian

## Ingredients
- 4 oz dark chocolate
- 4 tbsp butter
- 2 eggs
- 2 egg yolks
- 1/4 cup sugar
- 2 tbsp flour
- Vanilla ice cream

## Description
Warm molten center with cold vanilla ice cream. Must be ordered at start of meal.

## Instructions
1. Melt chocolate and butter together
2. Whisk eggs, yolks, and sugar until pale
3. Fold in chocolate mixture and flour
4. Bake at 425F for 12-14 minutes
5. Invert onto plate, serve immediately with ice cream

## Notes
Prep ramekins in advance during slow periods. 12 min = runny center, 14 min = soft center.
